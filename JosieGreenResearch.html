Capstone Research Project:

What are the effects of negative student-teacher relationships and what students are most at risk?


Initial Research
  1. Student-teacher relationships are influential in a child's academic and emotional development, and are more likely to be negitive for students of certain backgrounds.
  2. Schools need to find new ways of addressing behavoiral issues and better educate teachers on how to best support students in order to improve student teacher relationships.

Root Causes
  1. Lack of understanding and negative assumptions from teachers being made because of race lead to students of color more often being affected by negative student teacher relationships.
  2. Lack of understanding around and poor solutions to why students behave in a disruptive or unproductive way in the classroom is another leading factor in negative student-teacher relationships.
